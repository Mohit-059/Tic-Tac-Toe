About the Game

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a classic game enjoyed by people of all ages. It's a simple yet engaging game that requires strategic thinking and planning. The objective is to form a line of three of your symbols (either "X" or "O") horizontally, vertically, or diagonally on a grid.

Rules:

  • The game is played on a 3x3 grid.
  • Players take turns placing their symbol ("X" or "O") on an empty square.
  • The first player to create a line of three of their symbols wins the game.
  • If all squares are filled and no player has formed a line, the game is a draw.

Features:

  • Play against a friend or challenge the computer.
  • Choose your preferred symbol: "X" or "O".
  • Enjoy a sleek and modern design with intuitive gameplay.
  • Keep track of your wins, losses, and draws.
